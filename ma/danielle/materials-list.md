# Materials List — Week 1

Aggregate list across all 20 Week 1 lessons. Designed to be handed to a school admin, dropped into a Donors Choose project, or used as a check-out form before the program starts.

**Assumed class size: 20 students.** Adjust quantities as needed.

---

## Capstone Materials (used across all 5 weeks)

| Item | Quantity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cardboard trifolds | 20 (1 per student) | ~$3/each at Staples, OR cut from large appliance boxes for free |
| Construction paper | 1 ream, assorted colors | For panel mounting |
| Lined notebook paper | 2 reams | For drafts |
| Plain printer paper | 1 ream | For diagrams |
| Glue sticks | 20 | One per student |
| Scissors | 10 pairs | Shared |
| Fine-tip black markers | 20 | Hand-lettering panels |
| Colored markers / pencils | 6 sets | Shared across tables |
| Highlighters (3 colors) | 20 of each | For annotation lessons |

**Optional tech:** A phone with camera + free QR code generator (e.g., qr-code-generator.com) for students who want to add a pitch video link.

---

## Per-Subject Consumables

### ELA — Photocopied excerpts (one packet per student)
- *Miles Morales: Spider-Man* by Jason Reynolds — Chapter 1 (~3 pages)
- *Long Way Down* by Jason Reynolds — Will's opening monologue (~2 pages)
- *The Hate U Give* by Angie Thomas — Starr's home-voice vs. school-voice scene (~2 pages)
- *American Born Chinese* by Gene Luen Yang — first 4 pages (graphic novel)
- *Ms. Marvel #1* (Kamala Khan) — first 8 pages (comic)
- Two "before/after" sensory writing example paragraphs (teacher-made)

### Math — Printables (one set per pair or student)
- Hero Stat Cards handout (6 heroes × 4 stats each)
- Boston-area map photocopy with scale
- "Labors of Hercules" handout with weights/dimensions
- Stat sheet template (4 rows: Stat / Number / Unit / Comparable to)
- Probability worksheet
- Calculators — 20 (or allow phone calculators)
- Rulers — 10 shared

### Science
- **DNA Lab (per group of 3–4 — plan 5 groups):**
  - Strawberries — 5 (1 per group, plus 2 spare) — fresh or frozen
  - Zip-top sandwich bags — 5
  - Dish soap — small bottle
  - Table salt — small container
  - Rubbing alcohol (70% or higher) — 1 pint, pre-chilled
  - Coffee filters — 5
  - Clear plastic cups — 5
  - Wooden stir sticks / toothpicks — 1 box
  - Safety goggles — 20 if available
- **Genetics Lab:** Two dice per pair (10 pairs of dice), Punnett square worksheet
- **Natural Selection Sim:** Paper clips — 250 total (50/group × 5 groups), some painted a contrasting color; yarn cut to different lengths; stopwatch (phone OK)
- **Fear Lesson:** Brain diagram label-the-parts handout, fear scenario cards (printed)
- Science notebooks — 20

### History — Printables (one set per group of 3–4)
- **World Heroes Card Set** (6 cards): Anansi, Sun Wukong, Maui, Gilgamesh, Cú Chulainn, Mulan
- **American Comic Covers** (printed or projected): *Action Comics #1*, *Captain America #1*, *Wonder Woman #1*, *Fantastic Four #1*, *Fantastic Four #52*, *Ms. Marvel #1*, *Miles Morales: Ultimate Spider-Man #1* — plus historical context cards for each year
- **Harriet Tubman Primary Source Set** (3 documents): 1860s wanted-poster style notice, Frederick Douglass praise letter, modern textbook entry
- **MA Hero Cards** (6 cards): Crispus Attucks, Phillis Wheatley, Frederick Douglass's Boston years, William Lloyd Garrison, Mel King, Mum Bett / Elizabeth Freeman
- Massachusetts state map (large, for class display)
- Chart paper — 6 sheets
- Sticky notes (post-its) — 1 pad

| Category | Approx. cost (class of 20) |

| Art supplies (paper, markers, glue, scissors) | $80 |
